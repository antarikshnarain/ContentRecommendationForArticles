Curfew continues in U.P. towns 

Curfew continued in Azamgarh and Mubarakpur of Uttar Pradesh today although no untoward incidents were reported from the towns which witnessed communal clashes earlier this week , according to an official spokesman here .
Para-military forces have been deployed in strength in four police station areas of Azamgarh to maintain strict vigil in the wake of Thursday 's clashes , a spokesman in the DGP office here said .
