DMK wins municipal ward bypoll 

Mr. Mahendran ( DMK ) won the by-election to the 22nd ward of the Vellore municipal council by a cling-hanger finish , by just two votes , following a recount .
Mr. T. Mohan , commissioner of the Vellore municipality ordered a recount which resulted in Mr. Mahendran being declared elected by a margin of two votes .
Mahendran ( DMK ) - 729 declared elected ; V. Umadevi ( AIADMK ) - 727 ; T. R. Krishnaswamy ( TMC ) -75 .
