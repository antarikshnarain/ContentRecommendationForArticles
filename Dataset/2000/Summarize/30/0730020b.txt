Leko holds Kasparov 

In an encounter between two of the four undefeated players in the meet , Peter Leko of Hungary snatched a draw from Garry Kasparov of Russia as lead positions remained unchanged with draws in the eleventh round of the Corus chess tournament here at De Moriaan on Friday .
