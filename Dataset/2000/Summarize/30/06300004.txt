Forex reserves up $63m 

Foreign exchange reserves have risen by $ 69 millions in the week ended January 21 , 2000 to $ 35,170 millions , as foreign currency assets ( FCA ) have increased by $ 63 millions to $ 32,221 millions .
Special drawing rights ( SDR ) were also up by $ 6 millions , according to the latest weekly statistical supplement of the Reserve Bank of India .
FCAs have risen by $ 4,750 millions while gold and SDR holdings have declined by $ 76 millions and $ 38 millions respectively .
