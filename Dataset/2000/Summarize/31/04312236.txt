MGR-ADMK candidate for Aranthangi

The MGR-ADMK has nominated Mr. C. Anbarasan , Pudukottai district secretary , as candidate in the by-election Aranthangi for Assembly constituency .
Reacting to the TMC 's decision to support the AIADMK , he said it did not augur well for the TMC as it was formed in 1996 to fight the AIADMK 's corruption .
