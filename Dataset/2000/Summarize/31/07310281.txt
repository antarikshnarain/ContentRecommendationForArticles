A lively match, but it's the same old story in the end

Their top notch one day specialist batsmen Michael Bevan ( man of the match ) sustained his side 's winning streak with a typically aggressive effort either side of the rain that held up play for more than half-an- hour , but did not force a fresh target to be determined .
Australia 's fourth wicket pair of Bevan ( who has now become the highest scorer in the tri-series ) and Ponting were going great guns when Joshi had Ponting caught by Agarkar .
