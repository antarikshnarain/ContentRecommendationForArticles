Moopanar to make statement today

The TMC President , Mr. G. K. Moopanar , has said that he will make a detailed statement tomorrow on the stand taken by his party to support the AIADMK candidates in the three by-elections for the Assembly on February 17 .
Mr. Moopanar was responding to questions from presspersons on the TMC Whip , Mr. R. Chokkar , resigning his MLA post in protest against the decision of the TMC to support the AIADMK here today .
