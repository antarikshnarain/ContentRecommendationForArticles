From the land of caribou 

The 14 member team of Rotarians will be closely watching the fourth and final phase of the polio programme to be held with the support of the Rotary movement on January 23 .
Introducing the team , Ms. Rekha Shetty , District Governor , Rotary District 3230 , said they will study the programme in which a huge volunteer army , mobilised by Rotary will aid the State in administering vaccine and IEC activities .
Led by Ms. Allana Wenholz Bergh , District Governor , D5010 , the team will keep a close watch on the way polio immunisation will be conducted in Rotary district 3230 .
Of course we will help wherever we can , but the thrust is rather on learning from here' ' says Ms . Bergh , who appeared excited about the experience , 24 hours after she landed in Chennai .
