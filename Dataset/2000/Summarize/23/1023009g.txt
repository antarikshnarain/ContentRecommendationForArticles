Virgo

Sun sign Virgo ( Aug. 22 to Sept. 22 ) Students , career women , professionals , artists and commission agents will find the outlook change in their favour .
Those in the printing and stationary fields should seize this lucky week by the forelock and advance .
Moon in KANYA ( Stars - the last three quarters of Uttaraphalguni , Hashta and the first two quarters of Chitta ) Tides will flow in favour of women and artists .
A grown-up ward may cause you uneasiness because you are not able to read his or her mind .
