Libra

Sun sign Libra ( Sept. 23 to Oct. 22 ) A pleasure trip will give you a chance to get away from the normal rut and meet new faces .
Family celebrations will also see you in good cheer .
Shrewd handling of finances and tactful relations with business partners , friends and relatives will be to your advantage now .
Invest only small sums in race , lottery , etc .
Moon in THULA ( Stars - the last two quarters of Chitta , Swathi and the first three quarters of Visaka ) Students , professionals and commission traders will see their fortunes improve .
