Scorpio

Sun sign Scorpio ( Oct. 23 to Nov. 22 ) Finances may be in short supply .
You may be forced to locate tidy sums to take care of repairs to vehicles and property .
Do not worry if a child , a friend , a relative or a business associate is lukewarm ; deal with them patiently .
Moon in VRISCHIKA ( Stars - Last quarter of Visakha , Anuradha , Jyeshta ) Artists and the women are well-set to flourish .
