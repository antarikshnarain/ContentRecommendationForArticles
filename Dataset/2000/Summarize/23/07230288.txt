Arun Kumar's century pales before Kambli, Muzumdar's onslaught

Capitalising on curious cricketing deja vu , Vinod Kambli and Amol Muzumdar redeemed themselves and their team with a double century stand , as West Zone chased 317 to win its Deodhar Trophy match against South at the Municipal Stadium here today .
The sheer driving purpose of this third-wicket stand washed out an equally attractive century from South Zone opener J. Arun Kumar ( 121 , 121b , 13x4 , 2x6 ) in the morning .
