Striking Govt. staff clash with police in Rajasthan

At least 20 persons were injured in police lathicharge and stone-pelting by Rajasthan Government staff at Dholpur as the Gehlot Ministry hardened its stance against agitating employees whose strike entered the 38th day today .
The striking employees , who are demanding payment of bonus and additional instalments of dearness allowance , clashed with police at several places in the State .
