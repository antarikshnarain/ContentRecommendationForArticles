Pak. should be held accountable: Pallone

Condemning the hijacking of the Indian Airlines plane , the Democratic Congressman from New Jersey , Mr . Frank Pallone , has spoken of the need for increased India , U.S . partnership in identifying shared threats and in protecting their people .
India and the United States have both been particularly targeted by Osama bin Laden and the loose network of militant forces that share his goals .
This is a further indication of the need for increased partnership between the U.S. and India in identifying the shared threats we face and devising ways to protect our citizens in ways consistent with open , democratic societies,' ' Mr. Pallone said .
