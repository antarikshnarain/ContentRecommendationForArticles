'AIADMK will consult friendly parties'

The AIADMK will decide on entering into fresh political alliances for the Assembly by-elections only in consultation with ` ` friendly parties' ' , the AIADMK General Secretary , Ms. Jayalalitha , said today .
Asked if the AIADMK was worried about the delay on the part of the TMC and the Congress ( I ) in bringing down the Pondicherry Government , she said her party was not in any hurry for a change of government in the Union Territory .
