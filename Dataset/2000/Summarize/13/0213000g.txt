PM to chair meet on J&K; security 

According to well-placed sources in the Government , the meeting would also discuss in detail the counter-insurgency operations in the border State where militants have shown ` ` jehadi' ' fervour in the post-Kargil phase and ` ` suicide squads' ' have attacked camps of security forces and State police over the past two months .
Apart from the Prime Minister , the Union Home Minister , Mr. L . K. Advani , the Defence Minister , Mr. George Fernandes , the External Affairs Minister , Mr. Jaswant Singh would participate in the meeting .
It is learnt that the Centre is keen to evolve a multi-pronged strategy to effectively tackle militancy in the Valley and has also taken note of the recent statements of Maulana Masood Azhar , the Harkat-ul-Ansar ideologue , from Pakistan vowing to send more militants to the Valley .
