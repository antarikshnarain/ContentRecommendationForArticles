JD(U) to field candidate for Bellary LS bypoll

Mr. C. Byre Gowda , President of the State JD ( U ) unit , said that his party would field a candidate to contest the byelection to Bellary Lok Sabha seat .
Mr. Basavaraj Rayareddy , Mr. Tippanna and Mr. M. P. Prakash were among those whose names had been considered .
Mr. Gowda said that the BJP had agreed not to field its candidate in Bellary and had assured its cooperation .
