Army camp attacked 

SRINAGAR JAN 12 : In yet another ` ` suicidal' ' attack on security forces , the militants this evening barged into the sector headquarter of Rashtriya Rifles ( RR ) in south Kashmir town of Anantnag .
According to the DIG Anantnag-Pulwama range , Mr. Raja Aijaz Ali , a group of militants travelling in a gypsy and a Maruti van passed through the area where the RR headquarter as also his office is located and started firing indiscrimnately at around 7 p.m. today .
