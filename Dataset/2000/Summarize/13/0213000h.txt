Two arrested for possessing Masood's speech

Police have arrested two persons from Bihar , including a cleric of a mosque in Himachal Pradesh , for possessing audio cassettes containing speeches by Maulana Masood Azhar , one of the three militants freed in return for the hostages of the hijacked Indian Airlines plane .
Maulvi Mohammad Oyesi Ahmed , a cleric in a mosque near Palampur , and Zahir , a labourer , were arrested yesterday .
