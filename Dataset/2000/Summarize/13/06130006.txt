Dutch firm offers conversion kits 

Pioneers in building customised products for vehicles , Vialle has specially designed and developed a technologically advanced , high quality , safe and economical CNG and LPG alternative fuel system for the Maruti 800 car .
Speaking on the occasion , Mr. Hans Bruinsma , Managing Director , Vialle BV , Netherlands , said , ` ` With the constant depletion in the reserves of conventional fuel sources , the need to look for alternative sources of auto fuel has grown at a rapid pace .
' ' Vialle products will be available through Vialle Alternative Fuel Systems Private Limited , an Indo-Dutch joint venture between Vialle BV of the Netherlands and the Praja group of India .
