New variety of Pusa Bathua

Its edible portion has 60 per cent more vitamin C and 10 per cent B-carotene than locally-available bathua so far .
Tests have shown that the variety gives more yield than other local types.It can be grown on any type of soil but sandy loams are ideally- suited .
About 3-4 weeks old seedlings or attaining 6-8 cm .
Apply urea at 50 kg/ha in 3 dose after first , second and third cuttings .
Its first picking becomes ready 40-45 days after transplanting .
