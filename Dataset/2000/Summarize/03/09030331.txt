From Paris with love

A solo performance in Zingaro 's " Chimere " , participation in Pina Bausch 's dance show recently and Kuchipudi recitals at prestigious festivals the world over have won for Shanthala a special place in the dance firmament .
" The immense joy compares with nothing else , " says the young dancer , who is to perform in the city on January 7 , at 6-30 p.m. at the Rukmini Devi Cultural Centre , West Mambalam .
