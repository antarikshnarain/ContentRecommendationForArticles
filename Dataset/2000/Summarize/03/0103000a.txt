Oil pipeline blown up

GUWAHATI , JAN. 2.Militants of the United Liberation Front of Assam ( ULFA ) blew up a portion of an ONGC pipeline near Disang bridge in Assam 's Sibsagar district last night , causing oil spillage and fire .
The fire raged for an hour before it was doused , but no harm was done to the area , an isolated spot with no villages nearby , the Sibsagar Deputy Commissioner said .
