'Novelist of the century'

The author of the timeless classics , ` Charitraheen ' and ` Grihadaha ' , Sarat Chandra Chatterjee , has been chosen novelist of the century in an online poll conducted by a literary magazine .
The early 20th century novelist from Bengal polled 7456 votes while , Bhagwan S. Gidwani of ` The Sword of Tipu Sultan ' fame came a close second at 7,377 votes .
The well-known Hindi writer , Munshi Premchand , came fourth while India 's first Nobel laureate , Rabindranath Tagore , was fifth in the poll conducted by www.Meghdutam.com , the country 's first monthly English literary magazine on the net .
