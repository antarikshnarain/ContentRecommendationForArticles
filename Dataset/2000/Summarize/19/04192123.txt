MP Governor refutes Cong(I) charge

The Madhya Pradesh Governor , Dr. Bhai Mahavir , today refused to conduct a head count of the Bhopal Municipal Corporation ( BMC ) members produced before him by the local Congress ( I ) leaders and expressed anguish over the charge that he was acting in a partisan manner over the recent issue linked with the BMC Speaker 's election and the BJP corporators ' thrashing by the police during the election .
