Jaswant, Talbott begin talks

India and the United States today resumed their talks to narrow their differences on the Comprehensive Test Ban Treaty , disarmament and non-proliferation in the run up to the U.S. President , Mr. Bill Clinton 's visit to New Delhi , expected in March .
The External Affairs Minister , Mr. Jaswant Singh , and the U.S . Deputy Secretary of State , Mr. Strobe Talbott , began their two- day 10th round of parleys here in a veil of secrecy with officials from both sides unwilling to disclose the venue of the talks .
The issues being discussed by them related to disarmament and non-proliferation with emphasis on the CTBT , the Fissile Material Cut-off Treaty , control over exports of sensitive products and technologies and defence posture .
