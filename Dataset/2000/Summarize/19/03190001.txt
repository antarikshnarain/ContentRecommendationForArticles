Masood Azhar weds

He married late yesterday at his home in Bahawalpur , in central Pakistan , 100 Km south of here , his father , Allah Bakhsh Sabir , said .
Azhar , who has links to the ` Harkat-ul-Mujahideen ' group , appeared in public several times since his release and spoke at an Islamic seminary in Karachi earlier this month , vowing to wage a holy war against India .
