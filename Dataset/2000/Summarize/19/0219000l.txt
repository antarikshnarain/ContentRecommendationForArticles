Parties likely to go it alone in Manipur

The attempt by the political parties in Manipur to form two fronts ahead of the February elections seems to be an exercise in futility since most parties do not see eye to eye on seat adjustment .
The ruling party , the Manipur State Congress Party ( MSCP ) , in its bid to return to power , has been floating the idea of an alliance among NDA parties such as the BJP , the Samata party and the Federal Party of Manipur ( FPM ) .
The MSCP is planning to contest all the 60 seats , but the BJP national secretary in charge of the border States , Mr. P.B .
Acharya , told reporters that if the MSCP was against any seat adjustment , the Minister of State for Sports , Youth Affairs and Welfare Mr. Thounaojam Chaoba , the working president of the MSCP , should quit the Union Ministry .
