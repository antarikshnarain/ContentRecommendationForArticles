Respect Panchsheel, China tells India

China today hoped that India would ` ` fully respect' ' and ` ` implement' ' the principles of Panchsheel , guiding the Sino- India relations , while dealing with the 17th Karmapa Lama issue .
` ` We hope the Indian side would handle this issue in accordance with the principles of Panchsheel,' ' the Foreign Office spokesman , Mr. Zhu Bangzao , said when asked at a press conference about the Chinese stand on the fleeing of the 14-year- old Karmapa , Ugyen Trinley Dorji , from Tibet to India .
