Cong.-NCP panel to draft common minimum plan 

The ruling Congress ( I ) -NCP front in Maharashtra has formed a committee to prepare a common minimum programme for the Deshmukh Government .
The State presidents of all front constituents would be members of the committee , which would submit its report to the Chief Minister within a fortnight .
While the former had inquired into the Mumbai riots and serial blasts , the latter had probed the police firing at Ramabai Ambedkar Nagar .
