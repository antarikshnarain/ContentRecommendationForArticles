Two terrorists arrested

The Director-General of Police ( DGP ) , Mr. Sarabjit Singh , told mediapersons that Balbir Singh alias Bagicha Singh of Bachiwind village and Kabul Singh alias Kanta of Chack village in the Majitha police district were trained in handling explosives and weapons .
Security has been stepped up at all venues where the flag- hoisting ceremony would take place on Wednesday , particularly in the city where the Chief Minister , Mr. Parkash Singh Badal , who is high on the terrorist hit-list , would unfurl the tricolour .
