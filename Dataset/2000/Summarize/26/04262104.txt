Lankesh dead 

Though better known as the editor of the Kannada weekly named after himself , Lankesh Patrike , his interests were varied .
His Lankesh Patrike became known for its highly individualistic first person articles lampooning and exposing the weaknesses and failures of the politicians in power .
Though there were other Kannada journals in the past known for their barbed writings , Lankesh Patrike stood out as it shunned advertisements .
