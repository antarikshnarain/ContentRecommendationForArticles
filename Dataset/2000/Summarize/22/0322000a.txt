Jaswant calls on Sheikh Mohammed

The External Affairs Minister , Mr. Jaswant Singh , today took time off his schedule to call on the crown prince of Dubai and UAE Defence Minister , Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Almaktoum , to thank him for his mediation in the hijacking crisis .
Mr. Singh , who made an unscheduled halt at Abu Dhabi last night on his way from London to Muscat , reached Dubai and invited Sheikh Mohammed to visit India .
