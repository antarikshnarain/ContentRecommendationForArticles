6 die as boat capsizes in Mangalore 

Five schoolgirls and a 45-year-old man died when a boat capsized at Pilikula Nisarga Dhama Boating Centre here today .
The names of the dead were given as Sushma , Anusha , Ashwini , Raveena Vaz and Prajna Attavar , all of them class VIII students of St. Ann 's School .
According to police , the accident occurred at about 11 a.m. due to overloading of the only motor boat available at the tourist spot .
Mr. Kamal Panth , Superintendent of Police , Mr. J.R.Lobo , former Executive Director of the Pilikula Nisarga Dhama , and other officials , pacified the parents and relatives .
Rescue operations in progress at Pilikula Nisarga Dhama Boating Centre in Mangalore on Friday .
