Scheme to promote welfare of manual workers launched

All registered owners of motor vehicles operating road transport for passengers and goods will have to pay one per cent of the motor vehicles tax from January 1 , 2000 , towards the Tamil Nadu Manual Workers ' Social Security and Welfare Scheme .
The scheme will be operated by the Labour Department as per the Section 8 ( B ) ( 1 ) ( b ) of the Tamil Nadu Manual Workers ( Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Work ) Act of 1988 .
