PM sees closer ties with Russia

The Prime Minister , Mr. Atal Behari Vajpayee has expressed confidence that India and Russia would work closely in consolidating bilateral strategic partnership in the new millennium under the leadership of the acting Russian President , Mr. Vladimir Putin .
We regard you as a good friend of India and highly value your personal contribution to the strengthening of Indo-Russian ties,' ' Mr . Vajpayee said in a message to Mr. Putin , released by the Indian embassy here today .
