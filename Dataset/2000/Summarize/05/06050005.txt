Stocks remain volatile, Sensex hits historic high

Equities sustained their rally for the second successive day and lifted the benchmark to another a historic high at 5534 during extremely volatile trading on the Bombay Stock Exchange today as speculators continued to cheer a triumphant transition to the new millennium , anticipating sharp increase in foreign investments in the country .
The BSE sensitive index opened with a wide gap at 5533.98 but later fluctuated erratically in a wide range of 5533.98 and 5376.43 before closing with a new record at 5491.01 against yesterday 's close of 5375.11 , netting a rise of 115.90 points .
