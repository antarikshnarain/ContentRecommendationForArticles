Insurgent leader's release on parole sparks row

B. Vajpayee , and the Union Home Minister , Mr. L. K. Advani , reviewed the law and order situation in Manipur with the State Chief Minister , Mr. Wahengbam Nipamacha , and the Chief Secretary , Mr. H. Jelshyam , recently .
They also reportedly discussed the controversial release on parole , of the Kanglei Yawal Kunna Lup ( KYKL-O ) leader , N. Oken .
The decision was taken by the Chief Secretary without the Chief Minister 's knowlege Meanwhile , Oken had sent a letter to the Chief Secretary requesting three months ' extension of his parole .
Officials felt , more insurgents might apply for parole , if Oken was granted an extension .
