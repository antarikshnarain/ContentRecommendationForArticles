
Red alert for Republic Day


By Our Special Correspondent

NEW  DELHI, JAN. 25. On the eve of the Republic Day,  the  Centre 
today sounded a red alert in all the States, apprehending  strike 
by suicide squads.Home Ministry sources said keeping in view  the 
recent  strikes by suicide squads in Kashmir Valley and  recovery 
of explosives from Delhi and Jammu, all the States had been asked 
to  enforce  foolproof  security  arrangements  in  view  of  the 
Republic Day celebrations tomorrow.

The sources said law-enforcing agencies and security agencies had 
been asked to take serious note of the reports of infiltration of 
militants into the Kashmir Valley. The vulnerability of  Himachal
