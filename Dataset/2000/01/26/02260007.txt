
Be vigilant against threats to unity: PM 


NEW  DELHI, JAN. 25. The Prime Minister, Mr. A. B. Vajpayee,  has 
greeted the nation on the golden jubilee of the Republic  calling 
it a ``major milestone'' in its democratic march and urged people 
to  remain  vigilant against threats to the country's  unity  and 
integrity.

In  his  message, Mr. Vajpayee said ``at  this  historic  moment, 
India  looks  at  her  past with pride and  to  her  future  with 
confidence''.``On  this  day,  let us  re-dedicate  ourselves  to 
justice, liberty, equality and fraternity'' 

- PTI
