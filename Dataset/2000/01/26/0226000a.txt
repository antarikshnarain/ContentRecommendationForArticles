
List of gallantry award winners 


NEW  DELHI, JAN. 25. As many as 506 armed forces  personnel  have 
been  awarded gallantry and distinguished service awards  on  the 
occasion of the 51st Republic Day.

The list of decorations and awards includes one Ashoka Chakra, 24 
Param  Vishisht Seva Medals, four Kirti Chakras, one Uttam  Yuddh 
Seva  Medal,  two  bars (honoured for the  second  time)  to  Ati 
Vishisht Seva Medal, 44 Ati Vishisht Seva Medals, one Vir  Chakra 
and 19 Shaurya Chakras.

Five  Yudh  Seva Medals, one bar to Sena Medal  (gallantry),  113 
Sena Medals (gallantry), three Nao Sena Medals (gallantry), three 
Vayu Sena Medals (gallantry), 18 Sena Medals (devotion to  duty), 
eight  Nao Sena Medals (devotion to duty), one bar to  Vayu  Sena 
Medal (devotion to duty), 13 Vayu Sena Medals (devotion to duty), 
two bars to Vishisht Seva Medal and 88 Vishisht Seva Medals  have 
also been announced.

In addition, one Sarvottam Yuddh Seva Medal, one Maha Vir Chakra, 
one Uttar Yuddh Seva Medal, 17 Vir Chakras, 13 Yuddh Seva Medals, 
106 Sena Medals (gallantry) and 16 Sena Medals (devotion to duty) 
have also been announced for Operation Vijay. Besides these,  197 
`mention-in- despatches' have also been announced.
