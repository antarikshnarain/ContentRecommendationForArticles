
Directive on banning Rs. 500 notes 


BAHARAICH,  JAN.  25.  Directives had  been  issued  to  strictly 
implement the ban on currency notes of Rs. 500 denomination along 
the Indo-Nepal border, a senior Customs official said here today.

The  Government had taken a serious view of the rising  cases  of 
counterfeit notes of Rs. 500 denomination along the border areas, 
the SP (Customs), Mr. O. P. Singh, told PTI here.
