
TRAI ordinance


NEW DELHI, JAN. 25. The Government today issued an ordinance  for 
the  reconstitution of the Telecom Regulatory Authority of  India 
and  creation  of  a telecom  dispute  settlement  and  appellate 
tribunal.

The  12-page ordinance follows the Cabinet decision last week  to 
split  TRAI  into  two bodies  for  separate  administrative  and 
judicial functions. 

- PTI
