
Bomb blast in Imphal 


IMPHAL,  JAN.  22. Several persons were injured when  a  powerful 
bomb  exploded at the crowded Thoubal bus parking area here  this 
afternoon, official reports said.

The bomb exploded at 3.20 p.m. The intensity was so high that  it 
shook the Imphal town, reports said. Details are awaited. 

- PTI
