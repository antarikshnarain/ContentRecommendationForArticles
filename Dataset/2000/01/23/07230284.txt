
Agarkar not to play


MELBOURNE,  JAN.  22. Ajit Agarkar will not play  the  Carlton  & 
United  series matches against Pakistan (Jan. 25)  and  Australia 
(Jan. 26) at Adelaide. 

The Indian team's physiotherapist, Andrew Leipus, who put Agarkar 
through  a fitness test at the Bellerive Oval, Hobart on  Friday, 
said  the  seamer  had not fully  recovered  from  the  hamstring 
injury. 

- Our Special Correspondent
