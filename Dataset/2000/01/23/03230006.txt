
Nigerian President to visit India


ABUJA,  JAN. 22. The Nigerian President, Mr.  Olusegun  Obasanjo, 
will visit India next week and participate as Chief Guest in  the 
celebrations  marking  India's  51st anniversary  of  becoming  a 
republic. Mr. Obasanjo will leave Nigeria on Monday for the five-
day  visit  during  which  he will  hold  talks  with  the  Prime 
Minister, Mr. Atal Behari Vajpayee. 

- AFP
