
Ganguly upsets Barua


By Rakesh Rao

UDAIPUR,  JAN. 12. All along, Grandmaster Dibyendu Barua  thought 
the 16-year-old Calcutta-mate Surya Shekhar Ganguly was following 
his  footsteps, that is until the youngster stepped on  his  toes 
here on Wednesday. 

On  what  turned out to be a near-perfect day for  India's  young 
talents  in  the Asian chess championship,  K.  Sasikiran  fought 
brilliantly  to  hold  the  other  overnight  leader,   Vietnam's 
Grandmaster  Dao  Thien Hai, Koneru Humpy battled  her  way  past 
Bangladesh's  experienced Rani Hamid and Tania Sachdev  outwitted 
Saheli  Dhar,  but P. Harikrishna  allowed  International  Master 
Abhijit Kunte to turn a certain defeat into a dramatic victory.
