
S.S. Dawra, new Director, ED


By Our Special Correspondent

NEW  DELHI,  JAN. 18. Mr. S. S. Dawra, Managing Director  of  the 
Food Corporation of India, has been appointed the new Director of 
the Enforcement Directorate, official sources said here today.

An  IAS officer of the 1967 batch belonging to the Punjab  cadre, 
Mr.  Dawra  is  likely to take over on Thursday.  The  post  fell 
vacant after the then Enforcement Director, Mr. Indrajit  Khanna, 
was shifted as the Chief Secretary of Rajasthan.
