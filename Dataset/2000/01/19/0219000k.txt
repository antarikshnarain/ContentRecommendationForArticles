
Balayogi revamps LS press advisory panel


NEW  DELHI, JAN. 18. The Lok Sabha Speaker, Mr. G.M.C.  Balayogi, 
has reconstituted the Press Advisory Committee of the House.

Mr. P. Dev Kumar of the UNI is the chairman of the committee, Mr. 
Vinod  Sharma (Hindustan Times) vice-chairman, Mr. K.S.S.  Murthy 
(Malayala Manorama) secretary and Ms. Jayanti Goshal  (Bartaman), 
joint secretary.

The  members  of the committee are: Mr. Sri  Krishna  (PTI),  Mr. 
Rajeev  Pande (Univarta), Ms. Smita Gupta (Times of  India),  Ms. 
Neena Vyas (TheHindu), Ms. Arti Jerath (The Indian Express),  Mr. 
Keith  Flory  (The  Statesman), Mr.  Shrikant  Sharma  (Navbharat 
Times),  Mr.  B.  S. Arun (Deccan Herald), Mr.  S.  B.  Khandekar 
(Loksatta),  Mr. K. V. Prasad (Tribune), Mr. Ajay Sethia  (Dainik 
Navjyoti),  Mr.  Rashid  Kidwai (The Telegraph),  Mr.  Atul  Jain 
(Gujarat   Samachar),  Mr.  S.  Acharya  (Jagran),  Mr.   T.   R. 
Ramachandran  (Eenadu), Mr. Shams-ul-Huda Shams  (Daily  Pasban), 
Mr.  Rajdeep Sardesai (NDTV), Mr. Harihar Sarup  (Freelance)  and 
Mr. Shailesh Sharma (Lokmat Group). 

- UNI
