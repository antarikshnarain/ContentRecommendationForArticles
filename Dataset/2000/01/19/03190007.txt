
Russia asks Pak. to nab hijackers


MOSCOW, JAN.18. Russia today accused Paksitan of adopting  double 
standards  by  not nabbing the hijackers of the  Indian  Airlines 
flight IC-814 eventhough the law of the country has provision  of 
death penalty for the air pirates.

Russia asked Pakistan to nab the hijackers ``who found shelter on 
its  territory after their recent misadventure,'' the  `Voice  of 
Russia' said.
