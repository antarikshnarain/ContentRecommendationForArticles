
Rakesh Roshan shot at


MUMBAI,  JAN.  21 Mr. Rakesh  Roshan,  actor-turned-producer  and 
director,  was  shot  at today by  two  unidentified  persons  in 
suburban  Santa  Cruz while travelling in his car.  He  sustained 
bullet wounds on his left hand. His driver was also injured.  The 
assailants escaped on a motorcycle. 

- PTI
