
Bail extended for Laloo


NEW  DELHI,  JAN. 21. The Supreme Court today extended  by  three 
months  the bail granted earlier to Rashtriya Janata  Dal  chief, 
Mr.  Laloo Prasad Yadav, and 50 other accused in the  multi-crore 
fodder scam.

The three-judge bench comprising Mr. G. P. Nanavati, Mr.  Justice 
K.  T.  Thomas  and Mr. Justice S.S.M. Quadri did  so  after  the 
expiry of the period of bail granted earlier.
