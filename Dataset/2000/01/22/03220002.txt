
Masood Azhar leaves for Afghanistan


MULTAN, JAN 21. Maulana Masood Azhar, one of the three  Pakistani 
militants  freed to end the hijacking of an Indian Airlines  jet, 
has  left  for Afghanistan, his father Allah  Bakhsh  Sabir  said 
today.

A  day  after  getting  married on January  17  at  his  home  in 
Bahawalpur  in  central Pakistan, Azhar left for  Afghanistan  to 
meet  Mulla Mohammad Omar, supreme leader of the  ruling  Taliban 
Islamic militia, Sabir said.

Before leaving, Azhar met leaders of some fundamentalist  Islamic 
groups in Pakistan. 

- AFP
