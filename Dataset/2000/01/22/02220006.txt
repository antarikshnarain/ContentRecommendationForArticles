
Hindujas respond to plea to join probe


NEW   DELHI,  JAN.  21.  The  London-based  non-resident   Indian 
businessmen Hindujas have sent their response to a  communication 
from  the CBI asking them to ``join'' the probe into  the  Bofors 
payoffs case, official sources said today.

While  refusing to divulge the contents of the  Hindujas  letter, 
the sources said the reply, received by CBI on Tuesday, had  been 
sent for legal scrutiny.

- PTI
