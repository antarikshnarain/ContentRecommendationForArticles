
Curfew continues in U.P. towns 


LUCKNOW, JAN. 29. Curfew continued in Azamgarh and Mubarakpur  of 
Uttar Pradesh today although no untoward incidents were  reported 
from  the  towns which witnessed communal  clashes  earlier  this 
week, according to an official spokesman here. 

Para-military  forces  have  been deployed in  strength  in  four 
police station areas of Azamgarh to maintain strict vigil in  the 
wake  of Thursday's clashes, a spokesman in the DGP  office  here 
said. Senior officials were camping in the area. 

Altogether 38 people were arrested in connection with the clashes 
which left a policemen and three students injured, he said.
