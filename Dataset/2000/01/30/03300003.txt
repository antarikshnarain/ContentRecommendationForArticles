
India's assurance to Syria on Golan Heights


DAMASCUS,  JAN. 29. India has assured its consistent  support  to 
Syria,  which is holding talks with Israel for the return of  the 
Golan  Heights seized by Tel Aviv in the 1967  Arab-Israeli  war, 
for its sovereignty and territorial integrity.

``Accordingly,  we have called for an early return of  the  Golan 
Heights  in  conformity with the relevant  U.N.  resolutions  and 
land-for-peace  principle,''  the  Information  and  Broadcasting 
Minister, Mr. Arun Jaitley said.

He arrived here last evening on a three-day visit. 

- PTI
