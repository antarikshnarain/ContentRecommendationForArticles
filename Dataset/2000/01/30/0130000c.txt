
Two killed in Pak. blast


ISLAMABAD, JAN. 29. Two persons were killed and three injured  in 
a  powerful  bomb blast in the Sialkot city  of  Punjab  province 
today, a day after two explosions rocked Karachi killing four and 
injuring many.

The  explosion ripped through a bicycle repair shop in  the  busy 
Dahalo Wali area creating panic in the city, police said. 

- PTI.
