
Militant killed in encounter


SRINAGAR, JAN. 30. Security forces shot dead a Hizbul  Mujahideen 
militant suspected of carrying out surveillance for attacking BSF 
establishments here last night.

The  ultra  was  seen  moving  suspiciously  on  a  cycle.   When 
challenged,  he opened fire on a BSF party which gave him  a  hot 
chase  and gunned him down in a brief encounter. A pistol  and  a 
grenade were recovered from his possession.

The slain militant was involved in killing of a number of  police 
personnel and other militancy-related crimes. 

- PTI
