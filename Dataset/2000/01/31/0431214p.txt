
2 Manipur Ministers escape bid on life 


By Our Correspondent

IMPHAL, JAN.30. Two senior Manipur Ministers escaped attempts  on 
their lives by militants on Saturday night. Two militants threw a 
Chinese-made  grenade on the house of the State Higher  Education 
Minister,  Mr. Kumar Singh at Sagolband area here. Two  vehicles, 
including  the  Minister's car, were damaged in the  attack.  The 
sentry  opened  fire, forcing the ultras to retreat.  In  another 
incident  KYKL and NSCN (IM) men ambushed the Tourism and  Family 
Welfare  Minister, Mr. Mayanglangram Babu's motorcade in  Thoubal 
district, killing one.
