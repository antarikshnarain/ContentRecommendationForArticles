
Orissa Minister resigns


JEYPORE,   JAN.   30.  The  Orissa  Minister  for   Finance   and 
Panchayatiraj, Mr. Raghunath Patnaik, has resigned from the  post 
and  also from the primary membership of the Congress(I) when  he 
was denied the ticket for the coming Assembly elections.

He took the decision at a Congress(I) workers meeting at Tanukuva 
village  on  Sunday. About 700 of his supporters  also  quit  the 
party on the day to follow him. 

Mr.  Patnaik,  who was in the party since 1961 as  a  legislator, 
also announced he would be contesting as an independent.
